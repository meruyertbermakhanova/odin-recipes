Satay Chicken Recipe

Satay Chicken is a popular Southeast Asian dish featuring marinated chicken skewers grilled to perfection and served with a rich and creamy peanut sauce. It's a perfect blend of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh pieces
  • Peanut butter
  • Coconut milk
  • Soy sauce and lime juice
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with a mixture of soy sauce, lime juice, garlic, ginger, and spices for at least 1 hour;
  2. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers;
  3. Grill the chicken skewers until cooked through and slightly charred;
  4. Prepare the peanut sauce by combining peanut butter, coconut milk, soy sauce, and a bit of lime juice;
  5. Serve the grilled chicken skewers with the peanut sauce on the side.
