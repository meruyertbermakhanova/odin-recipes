Lasagna Recipe

One of the most loved foods in the world is finally here! This is a beautiful Italian Lasagna with layers of slow cooked Ragù Bolognese and Besciamella cheese sauce.

And if you’ve never tried a homemade one before, that needs to change! If you can make spaghetti bolognese, you can make lasagne. It just requires a wee bit more patience.

Ingredients

  • Onion, garlic, carrot and celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Smear a bit of meat sauce on the base first – stops the lasagna sheets from sliding around;
  2. Layer 1 – top with meat sauce, bit of white sauce
  3. Layer 2 – lay out more lasagna sheets, then top with more meat sauce and more white sauce
  4. Layer 3 – repeat again, lasagna sheets, meat sauce then white sauce
  5. Final Layer – lasagna sheets, remaining white sauce and sprinkle with grated cheese
