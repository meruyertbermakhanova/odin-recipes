Curry Chicken Recipe

This delicious curry chicken is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines tender chicken pieces with a rich blend of spices and coconut milk. Perfect for a comforting meal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Onion, garlic, and ginger
  • Curry powder and spices
  • Coconut milk
  • Vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant;
  2. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides;
  3. Stir in curry powder and spices, cooking for a few minutes;
  4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer;
  5. Add vegetables if desired and cook until chicken is tender;
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
